# Viral Format Bank

Recognizable YouTube/social formats that can be adapted for product launch hooks. The Hook Writer (Type 4) and Research Agent reference this file.

## Formats Already Used in Style Library

| Format | Used By | How |
|---|---|---|
| Vanity Fair Lie Detector | DepthFirst | "Is there anything you'd like to admit?" → raise announcement |
| MKBHD Blind Test | Moda, Icon | "Can you tell which was made by AI?" / "Which cost $1?" |

## Formats Available for Adaptation

1. **Jubilee "Odd One Out"** — Group tries to guess the outsider. Great for: products that identify/detect something hidden.
2. **Hot Ones Escalation** — Increasing intensity per round. Great for: products with escalating capability or tiered features.
3. **Cut "Lineup"** — Guess traits about strangers. Great for: products that analyze/assess/evaluate.
4. **73 Questions (Vogue)** — Fast Q&A walkthrough. Great for: rapid feature tour of a product.
5. **GQ "10 Things I Can't Live Without"** — Essential items showcase. Great for: tool suites, multi-feature products.
6. **Mr. Beast Challenge** — "Last to leave" / endurance with prize. Great for: products with measurable outcomes or cost savings.
7. **Fake Expert (Jubilee)** — Find who's faking expertise. Great for: AI-vs-human quality comparison.
8. **Chicken Shop Date** — Casual interview over food. Great for: founder personality, casual brand voice.
9. **Yes Theory "Ask a Stranger"** — Escalating dares/questions. Great for: products that do increasingly impressive things.
10. **Carpool Karaoke** — Casual drive + entertainment. Great for: mobile/on-the-go products.

## How to Match Format to Brand

Ask:
1. What's the product's most VISUAL/DEMONSTRABLE capability? → Match to a format that showcases it
2. What's the product's core TENSION? (AI vs human, old vs new, fake vs real) → Match to a format built on that tension
3. What format would the ICP RECOGNIZE immediately? → Use a format popular with the target audience

## Research Agent's Job

During YouTube and X research, actively look for:
- Viral videos using these formats in the brand's industry
- New formats not on this list that are trending
- Formats that could be twisted/subverted for the brand's hook

Add any new formats discovered to the research brief under "Format Ammunition."
