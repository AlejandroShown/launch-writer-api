# Giveaways Library — Style Reference

Every CTA/giveaway section from the production scripts. These drive engagement (comments, retweets) and are designed to go viral on their own.

---

## THE SACRED CTA FLOW

Every giveaway follows this exact order:
1. **Celebration framing** → "To celebrate [our launch / this launch / our anniversary]..."
2. **The free resource** → Specific, standalone valuable tool/dataset/guide
3. **"The right operator" line** → "The right [operator/person/creative] could use this to [massive outcome with numbers]"
4. **Platform CTAs** → X: "Retweet and comment '[word]'" / LinkedIn: "Comment '[word]'"

## TOOL GIVEAWAYS (free tool, scanner, agent, analyzer)

### Iris/Fin
"To celebrate our public launch, we're giving away the world's first AI agent that scans your QuickBooks, bank account transactions, and subscriptions to find savings automatically. The right operator could use this agent and immediately save 6 or 7 figures per year. X: Retweet and comment Iris, I'll DM it to you. LinkedIn: Comment Iris, and I'll DM it to you."

**Why it works:** "World's first AI agent that scans [3 specific systems]" = incredibly specific tool. "6 or 7 figures per year" = massive dollar outcome. Tool works even if you never buy Iris.

### Parker

Retweet + Comment 'Parker' and I'll send you a link. Comment 'Parker' and I'll send you the link."

The right Operator could use this tool and close six or seven figures in deals. X: RT and comment 'draftboard' I'll send you the link. LinkedIn: Comment 'Draftboard' and I'll send it to you."

**Why it works:** "Warm Intro Scanner" = named tool. "Type in your name and company" = frictionless. The promise is seeing your OWN network's hidden value — personalized to the user.

## DATA/RESOURCE GIVEAWAYS (spreadsheet, dataset, playbook, guide)

## BOLD CONFIDENCE GIVEAWAYS (guarantee/bet)

Mom: 'why are you bringing me into this.' As part of this launch you can try Marpipe for Free until your first ads are live."

**Why it works:** "$10k donation + apology video from mom" = absurd, memorable, shareable. The mom cameo is a screenshot moment. "Free until your first ads are live" = zero risk.

### Marpipe (Bonus)
"Need more? If you spend over $150K a month on ads we'll give you $10,000 in free TV ads on Universal when you sign up. Yes, completely free."

**Why it works:** Bonus tier for bigger spenders. "$10,000 in free TV ads on Universal" = unexpected, tangible, impressive partner.

## TRIGGER WORDS (1 word, brand-related, memorable)

| Script | Trigger Word | Why It Works |
|---|---|---|
| Iris | "Iris" | Brand name, simple |
| Parker | "Parker" | Brand name, simple |
| Icon | "icon" | Brand name, simple |
| Draftboard | "draftboard" | Brand name |
| Contra | "Contra" | Brand name |
| Owner | "Owner" | Brand name |
| DepthFirst | "depthfirst" | Brand name |
| Durable | "DURABLE" | Brand name, caps for emphasis |
| Meridian | "AI" | Category word (brand is about AI visibility) |
| Marpipe | "Real" | Unexpected — ties to "real physical gift" giveaway |
| Arcads | "AI" | Category word |
| Arcads (ALT) | "Editor" | Ties to editor spreadsheet giveaway |

**Pattern:** Default to brand name. Use category word ("AI") when brand name is less memorable or the giveaway is about the category not the brand. "Real" (Marpipe) works because the giveaway is a physical gift — the word mirrors the offering.
