## OPTIONAL BEATS (Include when brand warrants)

### Enemy/Pain (1-2 sentences, IMMEDIATELY into product)
"Right now, [pain with numbers]... [Product] solves this."
Only when brand has a CLEAR enemy. Mark: `OPTIONAL — INCLUDED/EXCLUDED because [reason]`

### Before/After (specific numbers, 10x+ gap)
"Before [Product], [old]. With [Product], [new]."
Only when gap is dramatic enough.

### Mission Alignment ("They X. We Y.")
"Other companies are using AI to replace you. We are using AI to make you the business owner." (Durable)
Only when brand fights a systemic wrong.

### Feature Expansion (flowing sentence, NOT bullets)
"[Product] also [X], [Y], and [Z]."
Only when major features weren't covered in demo.

## SACRED FLOW STARTERS

**USE:** "First, we'll...", "Right now, [Product] is...", "Here, [Product] notices...", "[Product] pulls/scans/analyzes...", "Next, we'll click...", "In [X] seconds/minutes...", "Let's ask [Product]:...", "We'll tell [Product]:..."

**NEVER:** "Connect your...", "Click the...", "Upload your...", "Open the..." (imperative commands)

**PIVOTS:** "Now we try something else.", "But that's just the start.", "But [Product] doesn't stop there.", "But [Product] doesn't just sit back and watch."
